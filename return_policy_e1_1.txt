EasyShop Return Policy

At EasyShop, we strive to provide our customers with the best possible shopping experience. However, in the unlikely event that you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a flexible return and refund policy.

Return Period:
You have 14 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return or exchange. This period starts on the day you receive your order.

Eligibility for Return:

The item must be in its original condition, with all original packaging, accessories, and documentation included.
The item must not have been worn, or damaged in any way.
The item must be returned within 14 days of delivery.

Return Methods:

You can initiate a return by contacting our customer service team via email at returns@easyshop.com. Please provide your order number, reason for return, and photos of the item(s) being returned.

Refund Process:

Refunds will be processed within 7 business days of receiving the returned item.
Refunds will be issued in the original payment method.

Exchange Policy:
If you would like to exchange your item for a different size, color, or model, please follow the same return process as above. We will provide a prepaid return shipping label, and once we receive the returned item, we will ship out the new item within 3-5 business days.

Defective or Damaged Items:
If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact our customer service immediately. We will provide a prepaid return shipping label and a replacement item as soon as possible.

Additional Tips:

Please ensure that all original packaging, accessories, and documentation are included in the returned item.
Any damage caused to the package during return shipping is not the responsibility of EasyShop.
Refunds will be processed within the 14-day return period.
By shopping on EasyShop.com, you acknowledge that you have read and understood our return policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us at returns@easyshop.com or through our website's contact form.
